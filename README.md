## Executive Summary

Healthcare organizations generate large volumes of operational and patient-related data every day. Converting this information into meaningful insights is essential for improving patient experience, optimizing hospital operations, and supporting strategic decision-making.

This interactive Tableau dashboard analyzes **2,200 patient visits** across multiple hospital branches, providing a comprehensive view of patient satisfaction, waiting time, consultation efficiency, departmental performance, and operational trends.

The dashboard enables hospital administrators to identify performance gaps, compare branches and departments, and prioritize areas requiring improvement.

---

## Business Problem

Hospital management needs timely and accurate insights to answer questions such as:

* Which departments deliver the highest patient satisfaction?
* Which hospital branches receive the highest patient volume?
* How does waiting time impact patient satisfaction?
* Which age groups report the best and worst experiences?
* Which operational factors most influence overall satisfaction?

Without centralized reporting, these insights remain difficult to identify quickly.

## Dashboard Features

### Executive KPI Cards

The dashboard highlights five key performance indicators:

* Total Patient Visits
* Average Patient Satisfaction Score
* Average Waiting Time
* Average Consultation Time
* Overall Satisfaction Percentage

---

### Department Performance Matrix

A heatmap comparing average satisfaction scores across hospital branches and medical departments, making it easy to identify high-performing and underperforming service areas.

---

### Patient Volume Analysis

Visual comparison of patient visits across hospital branches to understand workload distribution.

---

### Visit Type Experience

Comparison of patient satisfaction across:

* Walk-in Visits
* Appointment Visits
* Emergency Visits

---

### Age Group Experience

Evaluation of satisfaction levels among:

* Adults
* Children
* Senior Citizens

---

### Patient Experience Drivers

Comparison of key service quality indicators:

* Staff Behaviour Rating
* Overall Satisfaction Rating
* Facility Cleanliness Rating

---

### Department Efficiency Analysis

Scatter plot analyzing the relationship between:

* Waiting Time
* Patient Satisfaction

This visualization helps identify departments delivering efficient service while maintaining high satisfaction levels.

---

## Key Insights

✔ Patient satisfaction varies significantly across departments and hospital branches.

✔ Waiting time remains one of the strongest operational factors influencing patient experience.

✔ Some departments maintain high patient satisfaction despite handling larger patient volumes, indicating operational efficiency.

✔ Staff behaviour and facility cleanliness consistently contribute to improved patient satisfaction.

✔ Interactive filtering enables detailed performance analysis for specific hospitals, departments, age groups, and visit types.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Reduce waiting times in lower-performing departments.
* Adopt operational practices from high-performing branches.
* Continue investing in staff training and patient engagement initiatives.
* Improve resource allocation during peak patient traffic.
* Monitor department-level performance through continuous dashboard reporting.
